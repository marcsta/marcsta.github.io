Zen Water

The Zen Essentials® product line falls within the rapidly growing, alkaline water category. Unlike traditional waters, “alkaline lifestyle waters” such as Zen Essentials® have a broader appeal to the general public which is conscious of the health benefits. Zen Essentials® also captures Gen Z and Gen X consumers who support environment-friendly beverages through 100% Born-Again Ocean Bottles.

Listen Up Media

In 2018, Malcolm Jenkins started his production label, Listen Up Media, with the intent to “showcase, highlight and distribute content that mandates a conversation, drives engagement and creates social awareness around systemic issues in society.” The production company currently has several projects in development, including sketch comedy and scripted series, digital content, and documentaries, all focusing on systemic issues in society. Listen Up Media is currently in post production on Black Boys, a documentary examining black male identity and opportunity at the intersection of sports, education, and criminal justice.
